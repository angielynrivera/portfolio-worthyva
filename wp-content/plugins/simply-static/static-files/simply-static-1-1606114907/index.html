Have Any Questions?

How secure is my business and personal information?

Client confidentiality and security are of the utmost importance to me and I am always alert and cautious when handling client information. All data is organized and stored in the cloud. All information is kept confidential and will never be leaked or discussed with third parties. After your task is completed, I use a file shredder to permanently delete files that are no longer needed through a process known as wiping.

What is your turnaround time?

Turnaround time can depend on the complexity and urgency of the project or task. Smaller tasks are completed within 1 to 3 days. If you require urgent assistance, the deadline will be agreed upon before the work begins.

What are your hourly rates?

The most popular package, the Basic Package, is US$60 for 4 hours of professional support per month. If you require more than 4 hours per month, please call or email me to discuss your specific needs. We look forward to learning how we can assist you.
